Here are the questions grouped by category:

---

**Identity**
- What is the full name of the patient/claimant?
- What are any alternate spellings or names used for the patient in the documents?
- What is the patient's date of birth?
- What is the patient's gender?
- What is the patient's address or city of residence mentioned in the documents?

---

**Incident**
- What is the date of the alleged injury or incident?
- Where did the incident occur? (job site, address, employer location)
- What was the patient doing at the time of the incident? (job role, task)
- Who was the patient's employer at the time of the incident?
- Were there any witnesses to the incident mentioned in the documents?
- Was the incident reported to the employer? If yes, when?

---

**Injuries**
- What body parts are claimed to be injured?
- What is the nature of each claimed injury? (fracture, tear, strain, herniation etc.)
- Are any injuries described as pre-existing or degenerative in any document?
- Is there any contradiction between what different doctors say about the injuries?

---

**Medical Providers**
- Who are the treating physicians and what are their specialties?
- What clinics or hospitals has the patient visited?
- Has an Independent Medical Examination (IME) been conducted? By whom?
- Do any providers have a known bias noted in the documents? (referred by attorney, repeat appearances in claims)

---

**Legal**
- Who is the patient's attorney or law firm?
- Has a lawsuit or claim been formally filed?
- Are any other claimants, cases, or incidents referenced or cited in the documents?
- What is the claimed compensation or nature of the legal demand?
